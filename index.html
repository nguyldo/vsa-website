About Us

The Vietnamese Student Association at Purdue’s (VSAP) mission is to increase the cultural awareness and promotion of Vietnamese culture on campus, within the midwest, and across the nation.

Our membership comes together to implement social, cultural events, while encouraging a multimedia medium. We provide these opportunities for Vietnamese as well as non-Vietnamese individuals who are interested in experiencing Vietnamese culture, heritage, and tradition.

VSAP hosts a variety of social and cultural events throughout the school year, including Pho Night, Date Auction, and our annual Tet Lunar New Year Winter Formal!
